# 🍽️ Fork & Flame – Restaurant Menu UI/UX Design

A modern and user-friendly restaurant menu experience designed as part of the CodeVedX UI/UX Internship Program.

## 📌 Project Overview
Fork & Flame is a mobile restaurant menu application designed to provide customers 
with an engaging and seamless food browsing experience.
The design focuses on visual appeal, easy navigation, and well-organized menu categories to enhance usability and encourage ordering.

## ✨ Features
- Elegant dark-themed UI with premium gold accents
- Categorized menu sections for easy dish discovery
- Interactive food browsing experience
- Detailed dish information with descriptions and pricing
- Shopping cart and checkout interface
- User-centric and intuitive navigation

## 📱 Screens Included
- Splash / Welcome Screen
- Home & Featured Menu Screen
- Food Browsing & Search Screen
- Cart & Checkout Screen

## 🎯 Objectives Achieved
✔ Visually appealing design

✔ Easy-to-use interface

✔ Clear menu categorization

✔ Enhanced customer experience
